Add this hairdresser

You want to add a new hairdresser to the database but need some help?

1. Enter the hairdresser's name and the address

2. Then click on "Validate address" to get the coordinates. This is mandatory to display the hairdresser on the map!




3. Now enter the contact information. In case there are more phone numbers, e.g. mobile number then just click at the plus.





4. Here you enter the opening times. If the hairdresser is closed at midday for example, click at the plus and you get a second row with opening times for that specific day. If it's closed the whole day then click at the checkbox closed.















5. The prices are very important so please enter all of them. For hairdressers that only make haircuts WITH washing then just write the same price in the field for the price WITHOUT washing.




6. Further information can be entered in the text area here.





7. Confirm your input
